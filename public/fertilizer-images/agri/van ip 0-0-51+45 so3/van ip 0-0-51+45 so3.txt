Van Iperen SOP 0-0-51 + 45% SO₃ – Premium Sulphate of Potash Fertilizer

High Potassium | Sulfur Enriched | Chloride-Free Formula

Van Iperen 0-0-51 + 45% SO₃ is a top-quality Sulphate of Potash (SOP) fertilizer, offering 51% Potassium (K₂O) and 45% Sulfur Trioxide (SO₃). Designed for high-value and chloride-sensitive crops, it improves fruit size, color, taste, and overall crop quality. The added sulfur enhances protein synthesis and supports efficient nutrient uptake.

Fully water-soluble and safe for use in fertigation systems, this formulation is ideal for vegetables, fruits, tea, tobacco, and ornamental plants.

Application Method:
Apply via fertigation or soil application. Use according to crop-specific requirements or consult an agronomist for best results.
