ViviGro K – Sulphate of Potash (0-0-50 + 17% S)

High Potassium | Chloride-Free | Improves Crop Quality

ViviGro K is a premium-grade Sulphate of Potash (SOP) fertilizer containing 50% Potassium (K₂O) and 17% Sulfur (S). It is completely water-soluble and free from chloride, making it ideal for salt-sensitive crops such as fruits, vegetables, tea, and tobacco. The high potassium content supports better fruit size, taste, color, and shelf life, while sulfur improves protein synthesis and overall plant vigor.

Perfect for fertigation, foliar spray, or soil application, Vivi K promotes healthy plant growth and improves resistance to drought and disease.

Application Method:
Apply through fertigation or soil application as per crop requirements. Dosage may vary; consult with an agronomist for accurate recommendations.
