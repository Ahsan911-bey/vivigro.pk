Van Iperen Urea 17-44-0 – High-Phosphorus Fertilizer with Nitrogen

Strong Rooting | Acidifying Effect | High Solubility

Van Iperen Urea 17-44-0 is a fully water-soluble fertilizer combining 17% Nitrogen (N) and 44% Phosphorus (P₂O₅), ideal for early plant development and root establishment. Its acidic nature helps improve nutrient availability, especially in alkaline soils, and prevents clogging in fertigation systems.

Best suited for vegetables, fruits, and greenhouse crops, this formulation ensures fast nutrient uptake, improved flowering, and stronger root systems, leading to better yields and plant health.

Application Method:
Apply via fertigation or foliar spray based on crop needs. Consult with an agronomist for tailored recommendations.
